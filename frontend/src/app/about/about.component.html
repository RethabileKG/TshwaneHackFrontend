About Us

Welcome to the digital management platform for the City of Tshwane’s cultural, heritage, recreational, and museum facilities. Our solution is designed to make these valuable community assets more accessible, user-friendly, and efficient to manage.

Our Solution

We are dedicated to providing a modern platform that enhances the user experience while improving operational efficiency. Our system offers:

  • Real-time booking and availability management for easy space reservations.
  • Integrated payment processing for seamless and secure transactions.
  • Transparent pricing for all services, promoting clarity and ease of use.
  • AI-driven marketing to boost visibility and engagement with the City’s attractions.
  • Virtual tour technologies to give users a rich, interactive experience.
  • A loyalty rewards program to encourage repeat visits and user retention.
  • Automated reporting system for tracking usage statistics and performance metrics.
  • Categorized services for improved navigation and quick access to information.
  • Enhanced record management for staff to maintain accurate and up-to-date data.

Our Vision

Our platform will serve as a central hub, making it simple for both local residents and tourists to explore and enjoy Tshwane’s cultural heritage. By leveraging digital technologies, we aim to drive engagement, boost visibility, and contribute to the financial sustainability of the City’s public spaces.

Who We Serve

Our platform is designed for the residents of the City of Tshwane, cultural enthusiasts, recreational users, museum-goers, local businesses, students, and tourists. We aim to foster partnerships with local organizations to enhance the experience for all users.
